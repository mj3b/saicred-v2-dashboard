Three metrics to watch

Moral fidelity: Widest gap between top and bottom. o3 at 4.71, Claude at 4.16. Flagged as Critical for moral theology and apologetics use cases in the SAICRED white paper.

Source citation: GPT-5.4 at 2.41 — lowest in the field. 210 of 400 source citation scores are exactly 1. Responses are doctrinally accurate but cite no magisterial source.

Relativism resistance: Separates the field most cleanly. o3 at 4.76, Claude at 4.24. Directly drives the cap rate pattern in the Failure Explorer.

Framing pattern in Claude's failures

Claude's 61 relativism failures concentrate outside the Catholic framing: 23 neutral, 24 Christian, 14 adversarial, zero on Catholic. The Catholic context cue eliminates Claude's relativism failures entirely. Without it, Claude defaults to multi-perspective framing the judge reads as treating Catholic doctrine as one opinion among several.
